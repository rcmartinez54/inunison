Services

Individuals with developmental disabilities and behavior/emotional problems pose unique and complicated challenges. In Unison’s Level 4G programming is designed to achieve successful results in the areas of Behavior Management and Social Skills. The program seeks to significantly reduce all targeted maladaptive behaviors while replacing them with socially acceptable and responsible adult behaviors. The development of social skills and judgment will promote the ability of residents to effectively cope with and manage everyday life experiences.

Under the guidance of the facility’s Behavioral Consultant, a carefully designed and individualized behavioral intervention plan will be designed, implemented, and utilized with each resident. The facility’s Administrator, with the assistance of trained behavioral counselors will carry out the behavioral program, employing non-aversive behavioral services and techniques, functional skills training and appropriate socialization to make a positive difference in their futures.
